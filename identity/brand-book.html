01 · The Mark

A pulse line that resolves into a spark.

A heartbeat for the medicine. A four-point sparkle for the AI. One glyph, two readings.

02 · Clear space & sizing

Give it room. The math is simple.

Reserve breathing room equal to the tile's corner radius on all four sides. Minimum sizes preserve stroke integrity on shared-hosting render paths.

03 · Do's & Don'ts

Six rules. They're enough.

04 · Color

Teal for clarity. Ink for trust.

Primary is brand-700 (#0E7490). Pair with ink-900 for type and white for surfaces. Status colors stay strictly semantic — never decorative.
